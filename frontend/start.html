Start by asking what your site needs to do. If it just needs to show information about your business, Static is likely right. If you post frequently on social media and want that reflected live on your site, Semi-Dynamic. If you need to update content yourself, manage users, or sell products, you need Dynamic. When in doubt, tell us your goals — not the tier you think you need — and we'll recommend the most cost-effective option.

At minimum: your business name, preferred colour palette or brand guidelines, and the text content for each page. Photographs and logos should be provided in high resolution. If you don't have these yet, let us know — we can discuss timelines accordingly, or quote for basic copywriting assistance.
A 3–5 page Static site typically takes 7–14 days from deposit payment to delivery, assuming content is ready. Semi-Dynamic sites with social integrations: 14–21 days. Dynamic sites with CMS: 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. These timelines assume timely feedback from your side at each review stage.

Neither is required — you are free to manage the site yourself or hand it to any other developer.
