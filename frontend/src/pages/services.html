Services

What we build,
and why it matters.

Every feature in our catalogue exists for a business reason. This page explains what each one does for you — not just what it is technically.

Tier One

Static Websites

Pure hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No database, no server-side code. Content changes require a developer edit. The simplest, fastest, and most affordable option — from 4,000 BDT.

Best for: Portfolios, landing pages, event sites, business brochures, and any project where content is fixed and you do not need to log in and make changes yourself.
Strengths

Why it works well

Extremely fast load times. Very low hosting cost — deployable to GitHub Pages for free. No backend means no security attack surface. Simple and reliable in the long term.

Limitations

What to be aware of

Cannot auto-pull live content such as social feeds or news. No user accounts or e-commerce. A developer must edit HTML files to change any text or image after handover.

Capabilities

What is possible

Contact forms via Formspree, image galleries, testimonial sliders, manually-updated blog sections, client-side search, animations, and all UI components in our catalogue.

Tier Two

Semi-Dynamic Websites

Client-side API calls, third-party form services, and lightweight integrations — without the cost of a custom backend or database. Live features at a fraction of a full dynamic build. From 6,000 BDT.

Best for: Small businesses that post on Instagram or YouTube regularly, service providers who need contact form email notifications, and startups that want a modern connected presence without a high development budget.
Strengths

Why it works well

Live social media feeds, YouTube embeds, and contact form notifications without server costs. Faster and cheaper than a full dynamic build while feeling genuinely alive and current.

Limitations

What to be aware of

No user accounts, e-commerce, or custom admin panels. Social API keys are exposed client-side — this is communicated to all clients upfront with clear explanation and options.

Capabilities

What is possible

Instagram and Twitter feeds, YouTube latest-video embed, contact forms with email delivery, headless CMS blog (add-on), client-side search, and all UI components in the catalogue.

Tier Three

Dynamic Websites

A real backend, database, and admin panel. Content is managed through a CMS so you — or anyone on your team — can update the site without touching code. The most powerful tier for growing businesses. From 8,000 BDT.

Best for: Businesses that publish content regularly, manage users or members, sell products online, or operate a site that needs to evolve constantly. If you want full autonomy over your content, this is your tier.
Strengths

Why it works well

Self-managed content, user accounts, e-commerce, multi-language support, scheduled publishing, custom admin dashboards, and full server-side search capability.

Considerations

What to be aware of

Higher development cost and requires a paid server for hosting. More power also means more to manage — our maintenance plans are strongly recommended for dynamic sites.

Exclusive Features

Dynamic-only capabilities

User registration & login (8,000 BDT), E-commerce cart & checkout (from 15,000 BDT), Custom admin dashboard (10,000 BDT), Multi-language / i18n support (5,000 BDT).
