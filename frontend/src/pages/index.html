The Process

From first conversation
to live website

A clear four-step process with milestone-based payments, so you always know exactly what happens next.

01 — Enquiry

Initial Conversation

Tell us about your business and goals. We recommend the right tier and answer questions with no obligation to proceed.

02 — Quote

Written Proposal

You receive a detailed, itemised quote in BDT — no hidden fees. A deposit of 40–50% secures your place in the schedule.

03 — Build

Design & Development

Development begins. You see the design before any code is finalised. Two to three revision rounds are included in every project.

04 — Launch

Delivery & Handover

Final payment is made, the site goes live, and you receive all source files. Ongoing maintenance plans from 2,000 BDT/month.
